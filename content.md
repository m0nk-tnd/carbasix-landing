## 4. Problem section

### Section label

**Why current sourcing takes too much time**

### Section headline

**Too many tabs. Too much manual comparison. Too much spreadsheet work.**

### Section intro

**Most import workflows are still fragmented. Teams jump between marketplaces, compare listings manually, and calculate landed cost in separate spreadsheets before they can decide what is worth pursuing.**

### Problem cards

#### Card 1

**Too many marketplaces to check**
Sourcing teams lose time switching between sites, re-entering filters, and repeating the same search process across sources.

#### Card 2

**Manual comparison slows decisions**
Even when good options appear, comparing price, mileage, year, and source details across tabs is slow and error-prone.

#### Card 3

**Import cost is calculated separately**
Purchase price is only part of the decision. Duties, shipping, fees, and other landed costs are often estimated outside the workflow.

#### Card 4

**Weak-fit listings waste attention**
Without fast filtering and prioritization, teams spend time reviewing cars that do not match the actual sourcing target.

#### Card 5

**Good opportunities are harder to spot**
When information is scattered, it becomes harder to identify strong options quickly and act before someone else does.

## 11. FAQ section

### Section label

**FAQ**

### Section headline

**Questions before you try it?**

#### FAQ 1

**Which marketplaces are supported?**
CarBasix is built to work with multiple international vehicle sources and marketplaces. The exact source coverage can expand over time, and supported sources may vary by rollout stage.

#### FAQ 2

**Is CarBasix only for importers?**
No. CarBasix is primarily designed for importers, but it is also useful for car traders, resellers, brokers, and sourcing teams that need a faster way to review and evaluate listings.

#### FAQ 3

**Does it calculate total landed or import cost?**
Yes. CarBasix is designed to help users estimate the full landed cost, not just the listing price, so sourcing decisions can be made with better cost visibility.

#### FAQ 4

**Can I open the original listing?**
Yes. You can review listings inside CarBasix and follow links back to the original source when you need to validate details or continue the purchase workflow.

#### FAQ 5

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. CarBasix offers a free trial so you can evaluate the workflow and see how it fits your sourcing process.

#### FAQ 6

**Do I need to install anything?**
No. CarBasix is a web-based platform, so there is nothing to install to get started.

#### FAQ 7

**Which markets is it best for?**
CarBasix is especially relevant for teams working in vehicle import and resale workflows where cross-marketplace sourcing and landed cost estimation matter.

#### FAQ 8

**Can I save or mark listings for review later?**
Yes. CarBasix includes workflow features for saving, reviewing, and prioritizing listings so promising opportunities are easier to revisit.
